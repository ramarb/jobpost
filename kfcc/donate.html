OUR  VISION

Where there is no vision, the people perish. Proverbs 29:18

I. To experience the Power of God as we preach, share, take, and send God’s Word throughout;

II. To allow the Power of His Word to have an impact on this body and this community; locally, nationally,

and internationally;

III. To ensure that as many as we contact; we share the Road to Salvation through the Power of the spoken Word;

IV. To build a ministry upon the Power of the Word where we are unashamed of the Gospel of Christ,

to love one another as Christ loved us, and for our lives to exemplify Kingdom citizenship.

“For the preaching of the cross is to them that perish foolishness; but unto us which are saved it is the power of God”

(I Corinthians 1:18)

OUR  CORE VALUES

I. Evangelism: This is the process of sharing the gospel with the lost and winning them to Christ, thereby enabling them to enter the Kingdom of God. It is asking them to repent of their sins, put their faith in Christ for forgiveness of sins, accept the gift of eternal life, and to follow Him forever as Lord. (Matthew 28: 18-20)

II. Discipleship: A life long journey of obedience to Christ that transforms our personal values and behavior. Disciplining is the process of teaching the new citizens in the Kingdom of God to love, trust, and obey Him, and to teach them how to win and train others to do the same. (John 15:8)

III. Fellowship: Focuses on the intimate spiritual relationship that Christians share with God and other believers through their relationship with Jesus Christ. Ultimately the church is bound together not with creeds or confessions, not with programs and ministries, but with unity produced by the Holy Spirit and driven by God’s love for us and our love for Him and one another. (Philippians 2: 1-2)

IV. Ministry: This is meeting another person’s need in the name of Jesus, expressed as service to people inside the church family and expressed as missions to those outside the church with the resources that God provides. This is where we will also commit ourselves to the ministry of (missions). (Galatians 6:10)

V. Worship: Worship is any activity in which believers experience God in a meaningful, spiritually transforming way. It leads worshipers to a deeper appreciation for God, a better understanding of His ways, and a deeper commitment to Him. Worship brings us face to face with our Creator and draws us closer to His image. (John 4: 23-24)
